About Calisthenics

Calisthenics is a training style based on bodyweight exercises to develop strength, balance, coordination, and mobility.

  • Core strengthening
  • Full body functional strength
  • Flexibility & mobility
  • Beginner to advanced routines

Workout Routine

Exercise Sets Reps
Push-ups 3 15-20
Pull-ups 3 5-10
Dips 3 10-15
Squats 3 20-25

Nutrition Tips

  1. Eat enough protein for muscle recovery
  2. Prioritize complex carbs for energy
  3. Include fruits and vegetables daily
  4. Stay hydrated all day
